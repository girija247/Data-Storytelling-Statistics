# 📊 Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation Project

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the Superstore dataset with the objective of transforming raw transactional data into actionable business insights. Through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analysis, dashboard development, and statistical validation, the project identifies key revenue drivers, profitability trends, customer behavior patterns, and regional performance opportunities.

The final outcome is a business-focused data story that supports strategic decision-making through data-driven recommendations.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance across products, customers, and regions.
* Identify high-performing and underperforming business segments.
* Evaluate customer contribution and retention opportunities.
* Develop interactive dashboards for KPI monitoring.
* Validate key business findings using statistical hypothesis testing.
* Communicate insights through an executive-level data storytelling presentation.

---

### 5. Statistical Validation

To strengthen business conclusions, hypothesis testing was performed.

**Business Hypothesis**

**H₀ (Null Hypothesis):**
There is no significant difference in average profit between Technology and Furniture categories.

**H₁ (Alternative Hypothesis):**
Technology products generate significantly higher average profit than Furniture products.

**Statistical Test Used:**
Independent Sample T-Test

The results provided statistical evidence to support business decision-making and validate category-level profitability differences.

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue & Sales Performance

* Sales demonstrated a consistent upward trend over the analysis period.
* Revenue growth indicates strong market demand and customer engagement.

### Profitability Analysis

* Technology emerged as the most profitable category.
* Furniture generated strong revenue but lower profit margins.

### Regional Performance

* The West Region contributed the highest sales performance.
* Certain regions showed opportunities for targeted growth initiatives.

### Customer Analysis

* A relatively small group of customers contributed a significant portion of total revenue.
* Customer retention presents an opportunity for sustainable business growth.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategic recommendations are proposed:

### Increase Investment in High-Margin Products

Expand marketing and promotional efforts for Technology products to maximize profitability.

### Improve Furniture Profitability

Optimize operational costs, shipping expenses, and pricing strategies within the Furniture category.

### Strengthen Regional Growth Strategies

Implement targeted marketing campaigns in lower-performing regions to increase market penetration.

### Enhance Customer Retention

Develop loyalty programs and customer engagement initiatives focused on high-value customers.

### Improve Inventory Planning

Prioritize inventory management for products that demonstrate strong profitability and demand.
